How your gift works

When charity: water began, they made a bold promise: 100% of public donations go directly to fund sustainable clean water projects. Operations are funded separately by a small group of private donors.

Source: charity: water’s 100% Model.

What your donation makes possible

  • Community-led water solutions built with local partners
  • Monitoring and transparency with GPS coordinates and photos
  • Training and maintenance to keep systems running

The Jerrycan symbol

The yellow jerrycan represents the burden many families carry to collect water—and the hope of changing that story.

Ready to help?

Every gift moves us closer to a world where everyone has clean, safe water.
